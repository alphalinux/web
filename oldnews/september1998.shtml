September 23rd, 1998
I'm putting this site together to help those who are just starting out in the world of Alpha Linux, people like myself. I recently purchased a UDB/Multia for myself (though I've been using Intel Linux for about five years) and when I started looking around the Internet I was surprised that there was no central site for Linux Alpha information. So, that's why I started putting this together.

I have just started work on this, so things aren't perfect yet. I'm looking for suggestions and information as to how to improve the site. Please feel free to send me an e-mail with your ideas, comments and suggestions. I'm also looking for a smallish penguin logo for these pages. If anybody knows of a good one or would like to create an Alpha-Linux specific penguin then please let me know. I promise to give you credit.
