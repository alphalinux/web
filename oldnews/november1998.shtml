Old News....

Warning links in here may no longer be valid and they will not be updated.

November 26th 1998
Linus has released the next development kernel, version 2.1.130, also known as the 'basted turkey release'.

I've made some updates to the docs on compiling a kernel to reflect IDE performance in the recent kernels and various other changes.

November 23rd 1998
Adam C. Powell IV has started a mailing list for high performance work on the Alpha. More info is available on the High Performance page.

November 22th 1998
We've added some pages for information on DSP and Electrical Engineering on Alpha-Linux (thanks to Michael J. Graffam for this information).

November 19th 1998
Linus has released 2.1.129. You can read his comments on it, 2.2 can't be far away.

November 17th 1998
RedHat has released two security advisories. The first involves sysklogd and all versions of RedHat. The second relates to Samba and is a only problem with RedHat 5.2. Updates are available from updates.redhat.com.

Update: Samba packages for older releases of RedHat may also be vulnerable. Everybody is advised to upgrade to the latest RPMs.

November 16th 1998
Kernel version 2.0.36 has been released. You can get it from ftp.kernel.org , mirrors may take a while to update. November 14th 1998
RedHat has placed a new ramdisk image file in the Linux/Alpha 5.2 errata. For those of you receiving a SIG11 when selecting packages during installation you should download the updated image from RedHat.
