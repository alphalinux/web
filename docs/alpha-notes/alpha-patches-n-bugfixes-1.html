1. Introduction.

This document details what parts of the Redhat alpha 4.2 distribution need repair, and why. It covers patches needed for the kernel, gcc, and to make profiling work. I don't know how much of this applies to Redhat 5.x - probably not much. Thus, this is mostly useful for those who for one reason or another choose not to upgrade to 5.x.

Someone in the know please check this for ACCURACY!!!

Thanks to Dominique Quatravaux for his excellent AXP/Linux port FAQ. It explains many of these issues in detail. You should read that too. It's always better to have two sources...

Thanks to Richard Henderson for explaining many of the additional points on which I was confused.
